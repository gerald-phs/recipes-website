Ingredients:

Process:

  1. Pan. Olivol in, garlic chopped. Garlic in.
  2. Fry fry fry. Smells outstanding. Garlic out.
  3. Rice in. Fry fry. Diminish heat.
  4. Water in. Cover rice and a finger's worth more.
  5. Rice absorbs water, acquires jouissance. Too much of that, and the rice turns into porridge. To little, and it's like chewing on small rocks.
  6. The gods are waiting. Courage required ahead;
  7. Tap water in finger. Wet finger abundantly.

    2. SEH
    3. BROM
  8. Heat diminish to 0. In one swift motion, briefly touch the edge of the pan with the wet finger. Duration: 9 picoseconds. DO NOT TOUCH IT WITH DRY FINGER. DO NOT ALTER THE RUNES: 9 picoseconds EXACTLY.
  9. There are two possible outcomes: (a) the water touches the edge of the pan and vaporizes instantly. Rice ready! or (b) the water touches the edge of the pan but does not vaporize at all. There is still water present in rice. Rice not ready. Wait a little longer!
